3. Alternating Schedule (24H)

For high fever, you can alternate Paracetamol and Ibuprofen to safely maintain fever control.

  • Hour 0: Paracetamol
  • Hour 3: Ibuprofen
  • Hour 6: Paracetamol
  • Hour 9: Ibuprofen
  • Hour 12: Paracetamol
  • Hour 15: Ibuprofen
  • Hour 18: Paracetamol
* Paracetamol: Max 4 doses in 24h. Min 4h apart.
* Ibuprofen: Max 3 doses in 24h. Min 6h apart. Take after food.
